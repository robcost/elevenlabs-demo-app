## About Meridian

Meridian Member Services is an Australian and New Zealand provider of personal insurance and member-services support. Members hold one or more cover products and contact Meridian for product questions, claims, billing, and general support.

Business hours are **Monday to Friday, 8:00am–8:00pm AEST**, and **Saturday, 9:00am–5:00pm AEST**. Meridian is closed on Sundays and national public holidays.

## Member identification

Every member has a **member ID** in the format **M followed by eight digits** (for example, M12345678). Before any account-specific information is shared, a member must verify their **full name, date of birth, and member ID**.

## Cover products

### Meridian Life Protect (life insurance)
Lump-sum cover paid to nominated beneficiaries on death or terminal illness. Cover amounts from $50,000 to $1,500,000. Accidental death is covered from day one; cover for death by any other cause begins after a **13-month qualifying period**.

### Meridian Income Assist (income protection)
Pays a monthly benefit of up to **70% of regular income** if a member cannot work due to illness or injury. Members choose a **waiting period** of 14, 30, or 90 days before benefits begin, and a benefit period of 2 years or to age 65.

### Meridian Final Expenses (funeral cover)
Lump-sum cover from $5,000 to $25,000 to help with funeral and final costs. Accidental death is covered immediately; death by other causes is covered after a **12-month waiting period**. Premiums are fixed and do not increase with age.

### Meridian Pet Care (pet insurance)
Contributes to eligible vet costs for cats and dogs. Waiting periods: **2 days for accidents, 14 days for illness, and 6 months for cruciate-ligament conditions**. Annual benefit limits depend on the chosen plan (Essentials, Plus, or Premier).

## Cooling-off period

New members have a **30-day cooling-off period** from the policy start date. If they cancel within this window and have not made a claim, they receive a full refund of premiums paid.

## Eligibility

Cover is available to ANZ residents aged 18–65 at application (18–75 for Final Expenses). Pet Care is available for cats and dogs aged 8 weeks and over.

## What Ava can and cannot do

Ava can explain products, eligibility, waiting periods, and the claims and billing processes. Ava cannot provide personal financial advice or recommend which product a member should buy — those questions are transferred to a licensed specialist.
