# Meridian Member Services — Premiums & Billing

> Fictional demo content for the "Meridian" scenario. Knowledge-base document 3 of 4.

## Paying premiums

Members can pay premiums **fortnightly, monthly, or annually**. Accepted payment methods are direct debit from a bank account, and credit or debit card. Annual payment attracts a **4% discount** compared with paying monthly.

## Premium changes

- **Life Protect** and **Income Assist** premiums are **stepped** by default — they increase each year as the member gets older. A **level premium** option is available on request.
- **Final Expenses** premiums are **fixed for life** and do not increase with age.
- **Pet Care** premiums are reviewed annually and may change based on the pet's age and the chosen plan.

Meridian gives **at least 30 days' written notice** before any premium change takes effect.

## Missed payments and grace period

If a premium payment fails, Meridian retries and notifies the member. There is a **30-day grace period** during which cover continues. If payment is not received within **60 days**, the policy **lapses** and cover ends.

A lapsed policy can usually be **reinstated within 90 days** of lapsing by paying the outstanding premiums, though Income Assist and Life Protect reinstatements may require updated health information.

## Financial hardship

Members experiencing financial hardship may be eligible for a **premium pause of up to 3 months**, or a reduced level of cover to lower premiums. Hardship requests should be handled sensitively and transferred to a specialist — Ava should acknowledge the situation, reassure the member, and offer a transfer or callback rather than making a decision.

## Updating payment details

Changing bank or card details is account-specific and requires identity verification. If the member cannot be verified, or wants to change the policy owner or beneficiaries, arrange a callback with a specialist using **book_callback(phone, preferred_time)**.

## Refunds

Refunds apply during the **30-day cooling-off period** (full refund if no claim was made) and for any overpaid premiums after cancellation. Refunds are paid to the original payment method within **10 business days**.
