# Meridian Member Services — Contact, Callbacks & Complaints

> Fictional demo content for the "Meridian" scenario. Knowledge-base document 4 of 4.

## Contact hours

Meridian's member-services team is available **Monday to Friday, 8:00am–8:00pm AEST**, and **Saturday, 9:00am–5:00pm AEST**. Closed Sundays and national public holidays. Callbacks and transfers can only be scheduled within these hours.

## Booking a callback

When a member wants a specialist to call them — or when Ava cannot fully resolve a request — arrange a callback using **book_callback(phone, preferred_time)**. Confirm the **phone number** and a **preferred time within business hours**, and read both back to the member. Callbacks are normally made within **one business day** of the requested time.

## Escalating to a specialist

When a member needs a human specialist — for example:

- complaints or disputes,
- complex, sensitive, or declined claims,
- financial hardship,
- changes to policy ownership or beneficiaries,
- any situation where the member is distressed or vulnerable —

arrange for a specialist to call them back as a priority, and reassure them they won't have to start over. (In a live telephony deployment this could instead be a warm transfer to a human operator; in this service it is handled as a priority callback.)

## Complaints process

Meridian aims to resolve complaints fairly and quickly:

1. **Acknowledge** the complaint immediately and log it.
2. Meridian provides a substantive response within **5 business days**.
3. If the member is not satisfied, the complaint is escalated to Meridian's **internal dispute resolution** team, which responds within **30 calendar days**.
4. If still unresolved, the member can contact the **Australian Financial Complaints Authority (AFCA)**, a free and independent external dispute-resolution service. New Zealand members can contact the **Insurance & Financial Services Ombudsman (IFSO)**.

## Privacy and identity

Meridian only discusses account details with a verified member. Identity is verified using **full name, date of birth, and member ID** (format M followed by eight digits). General product and process questions can be answered without verification; anything account-specific cannot. Member information is handled in line with Meridian's Privacy Policy and the Australian Privacy Principles.

## Accessibility and support

Members who need an interpreter, or who have accessibility needs, can ask at any time and Ava will arrange appropriate support or transfer to a specialist team.
